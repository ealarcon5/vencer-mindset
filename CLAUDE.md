## Brand Vocabulary (use this language, not generic marketing language)

- **Vencer** = to overcome. Not a brand name — a direction.
- **Noise** = the functional layer, AI-replaceable content, hustle tactics, loud marketing
- **Signal** = depth, personification, identity, landmark content
- **World** = the universe being built around Emiliano's identity (not a "brand" or "channel")
- **Citizens** = the right people who belong in his world
- **Landmarks** = permanent content that claims territory and creates resonance
- **Sovereign** = operating on your own terms — no algorithm dependency, no hustle, no sales calls
